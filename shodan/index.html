What is Shodan and Why Should You Care?

Shodan is often called the "search engine for the Internet of Things (IoT)." Unlike Google, which searches for web pages, Shodan scans the internet for devices that are connected to it ‚Äì things like webcams, routers, servers, traffic lights, and even smart home appliances.

This exercise helps us understand that many devices are online and, if not properly secured, can be easily found and potentially accessed by anyone using a tool like Shodan. It highlights the importance of securing *your own* connected devices.
